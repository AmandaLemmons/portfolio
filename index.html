About Me

I am a learning enthusiast and rails developer.

I started my professional career doing Corporate Marketing where I was lightly exposed to the power of programming and data analysis. After teaching myself the basics, I decided a career change was in order and I enrolled at The Iron Yard to learn the ins and outs of Rails Engineering.

A little bit more… I graduated from The University of Texas at Austin with a degree in Corporate Communications and a Minor in Business. After graduation, I lived and worked in Mallorca, Spain for five years where I developed a love for tapas and rock climbing. In my free time I enjoy running, reading historical fiction novels, and trying to teach my dog how to properly play fetch.
